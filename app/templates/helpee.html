Grocery Heroes provides an internet platform for connecting higher risk demographics with lower risk volunteers. In providing your information to Grocery Heroes to request a delivery, you agree to have your phone number and location made publicly available on the Grocery Heroes website such that volunteers in your neighborhood can contact you regarding the fulfillment of your order. Grocery Heroes does not screen or perform background checks on volunteers that use the website; anyone can be a volunteer. Grocery Heroes does not take responsibility for the actions of volunteers. Please read through the following tips and requirements. By clicking I accept, you agree to abide by the following:

  • Although we assume the best of our neighborhood volunteers, we want to remind everyone to take all safety and health precautions at all times. Make sure to use your best judgement to prevent scam artists.
  • Please do not give out any additional personal information to your helper besides phone number and address for your grocery delivery.
  • Keep your door closed and locked. If you cannot pay your helper through an electronic method, slide your physical payment through a mail slot or leave it outside before your helper arrives.
  • I accept all risk and responsibility and will not hold any founders or representatives of GH responsible for any harm.
