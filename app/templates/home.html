Why Grocery Heroes?

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many seniors and other at risk persons are faced with a difficult decision: risk a trip to the grocery store or go without necessary provisions. This website is a simple way to indicate if you need help getting groceries, or offer your help if youâ€™re less at risk or already getting groceries anyways.

How does this work?

If you are part of an at-risk population and in need of assistance, we will first request your phone number and location. The request will show up for the Heroes to see, and they will contact you to ask for your grocery list. The Hero then clears the request, gets the groceries, and will drop them off at your doorstep. All you need to do is reimburse them digitally through PayPal or Venmo!

We do not permanently store any personal information.

Who are we?

We are a couple of UC Berkeley students trying to make things a little better in some very trying times.

Feel free to contact us if you have any feedback or suggestions!
